TITLE

Email Usage Policy

================================================================

1. PURPOSE

Protect enterprise email communications.

This policy establishes the standards and procedures to ensure the secure management of enterprise resources while maintaining compliance with organizational security requirements.

================================================================

2. SCOPE

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, interns, consultants, third-party vendors, and any individual who has authorized access to NovaTech Solutions resources.

================================================================

3. DEFINITIONS

- Enterprise Asset: Any digital or physical organizational resource.
- Authorized User: A user approved to access enterprise systems.
- Information Security Team: Team responsible for enforcing security policies.

================================================================

4. POLICY STATEMENTS

1. Validate Incoming Email must be archived.
2. Validate Incoming Email must be scanned for malware.
3. Validate Incoming Email communication policy is optional.

================================================================

5. RESPONSIBILITIES

- Employees must follow all security policies.
- Managers must approve requests where applicable.
- IT Team is responsible for policy implementation.
- Security Team monitors policy compliance.

================================================================

6. EXCEPTIONS

- Emergency requests require Security Operations Center approval.
- Business-critical exceptions does not require documentation.

================================================================

7. COMPLIANCE

- ISO 27001
- NIST Cybersecurity Framework
- Company Information Security Policy

================================================================

8. REVIEW CYCLE

Every 12 months
